Welcome to Hot Beans!

Our Features!

The most important part, baking the beans! We provide the most up to date baking process and we dont go light on the sauce! If you decide to take your training with Hot Beans you will not be dissapointed. We have a dedicated team of bakers who's sole purpose is to bring forth the next generation of beans.
Whether you prefer to be baked in a pot or left in the microwave, we have all sorts of methods to explore.

Here at Hot Beans we believe collaboration is the most important part of the baking process. Here we aim to bring the pod together and provide resources for our future beans! We host courses from various websites like W3Schools. To help grow the pod and bake more high quality beans, make sure to check them out!

We also have courses from other beans like Codemy and FreeCodeCamp who are really dedicated to growing our future generation of beans! Be sure to check out all peas in our pod and be sure to show them your sauce!

At Hot Beans we understand the importance of a good sauce, a final sequence in the process for baking our beans. We provide a variety of resources to help you get more comfortable in your sauce and to never let it simmer! We do this by giving you a collection of strategies for interviews, both technical and formal!
